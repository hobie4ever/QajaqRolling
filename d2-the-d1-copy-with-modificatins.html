D2 The D1 "copy" with modifications.

A friend asked to buy the D1 so I started building a "copy" in the winter/spring of 2017. Of course there are some modifications as you can see on the images. The cockpit is smaller and the masik sits a bit higher. Yes, the D2 isn't quite finished in the images above, the deckriggings are missing. I loved this qajaq and paddled it for four years. 
It wasn't so much that I wanted to do modifications as the itch to start building again that decided me to sell D2. Selling wasn't an easy task, the qajaq is "mine" for a number of reasons, nothing adjustable, no seat, no backrest, no hatches, no room for gear. My legs and knees determine the fixed positions of the footrest and masik and thighsupport so the person buying it need to be able to fit. I put out ads summer and autumn but a very mild response. Eventually I built a D3 and I ended up giving the D2 away to a friend. After all, it has been frequently used year around for four years and is a very special qajaq. But the advertising had reached a small group of interested people, more to come...
