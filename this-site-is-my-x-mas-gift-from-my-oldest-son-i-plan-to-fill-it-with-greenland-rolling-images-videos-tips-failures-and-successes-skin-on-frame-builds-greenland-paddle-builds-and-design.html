This site is my x-mas gift from my oldest son. I plan to fill it with Greenland Rolling images, videos, tips, failures and successes. Skin on frame builds, Greenland paddle builds and design.

As you may know there is a website called Qajaq rolls hosted by Cristopher Crowhurst which is a great site not to be confused with mine. I hope to be a bit more personal and I like to share my path towards completing the rolls on the Greenland Rolling competition list as well as discussions on Skin on frame building - what works for me and my mistakes. 

As my Swedish friends may be a bit annoyed by the English I start out this way and may turn bi-lingual as time passes. Any audio will most likely be in Swedish...
